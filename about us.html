IDENTITY

Talisay City College, a government-funded institution, is dedicated to delivering quality, value-laden, and affordable education. It actively engages in community outreach and research initiatives that drive socio-economic development. With a focus on holistic curricular programs, the college aims to be a globally competitive academic institution.

MISSION

Talisay City College’s mission is propelled by the institution’s commitment to provide quality and value-laden education through affordable tertiary education, be actively involved in community outreach projects aligned in the College’s thrusts, and engage in research-based activities relevant to socio-economic development.

VISION

TALISAY CITY COLLEGE is a globally competitive academic institution committed to meet the needs and challenges of times through excellent implementation of holistic curricular programs.
