The Talisay City College is made up of eleven under degree-granting, academic schools.
While The Talisay City College has its own personality and area of expertise,
TCC frequently work together to offer interdisciplinary degree programs and undertake complex research.



Bachelor of Elementary Education

Prepares future educators to teach elementary-level students, focusing on child development, teaching methodologies, and creating engaging learning environments.

Bachelor of Physical Education

Trains students in sports science, coaching techniques, physical fitness, and health education, preparing them for careers in athletics, coaching, and wellness.

Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management

Provides comprehensive training in hotel operations, tourism services, event management, and customer service within the hospitality industry.

Bachelor of Science in Industrial Technology

Equips students with technical skills in manufacturing, industrial processes, equipment maintenance, and technological innovation for various industrial sectors.

Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management

Develops expertise in tourism planning, destination management, travel operations, cultural appreciation, and sustainable tourism development.

Bachelor of Arts in Political Science

Explores political systems, governance, public policy, international relations, and prepares students for careers in government, diplomacy, and social research.
